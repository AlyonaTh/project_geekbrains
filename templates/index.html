Our History

In 2024, a web resource dedicated to home design and interior design was developed. Its main purpose is to provide information related to this topic, as well as informative materials and interesting facts. The site is designed in the format of a blog, giving visitors the opportunity to study the topic in depth.

The portal also has a section with affiliate links, where products corresponding to the theme of the web resource are selected. This section offers visitors the opportunity to familiarize themselves with quality products that harmoniously fit into the context of design and interior design. Thus, the site becomes not only a source of valuable information, but also a platform for familiarizing with quality products that support the concepts presented on it.
